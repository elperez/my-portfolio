About Me

I am a Full Stack Web Developer on a never-ending quest to improve my skills, learn new technologies and improve my craft in the web development space.

As someone who has excelled in maths and sciences and who has been facinated with technologies, taking up Computer Science in 2002 was the best choice for me. From 2006-2014, I’ve worked for Canon and Nokia in Manila, Philippines as a Software Engineer, and have attained the roles Project Lead, and Scrum Master working on Windows application and back end development. I always like to challenge myself and learn new things. This has given me the opportunity to travel overseas in Tokyo Japan in 2010.

Recently, I made a leap into web development and I’m eager to work on real world problems. I consider myself to be strong in problem solving, investigating, designing, and implementing feature enhancements. I love collaborating and have a penchant for improving existing processes by learning and trying out best practices, automation tools, and new technologies. My other areas of expertise include Object Oriented Analysis and Design, Agile SCRUM methodologies, and Test Driven Development.

The recent programming languages and libraries that I have used include JavaScript, Ruby, Node.JS, HTML, CSS, and Postgresql. Some of the web application libraries and frameworks I use are React.JS, Node.JS, Express.JS, and Rails.
